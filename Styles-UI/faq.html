How long does the program take to complete?

The program is flexibly-paced within a 7-month duration. There are three deadlines along the way that we’ve put in place to help keep you on track for graduation. Expect to devote a minimum of 15-20 hours per week to graduate within that maximum time frame. This is considered part-time study, and matches the default pacing of the program. If you’d like to graduate in as little as three months, you can devote 30-40 hours per week to reach that goal.
